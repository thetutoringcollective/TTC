Our Philosophy

Here at The Tutoring Collective we believe each student is unique and has their own story and needs, therefore their tutoring plan should also be unique and tailored to them. Designing the perfect tutoring plan is very important to us and we try to learn as much about our students as possible and applying this knowledge to our EPOU philosophy

Ensuring an engaging learning environment is crucial for any tutor and we always strive to learn more about our students' lives outside of the classroom so we can incorporate it inside the classroom. This allows for trust to be built and for more dynamic and personalized tutoring plans to be developed which motivates students to participate and build confidence.

Keeping a track of the progress in learning is key since this allows for a timeline to be built of the topics covered as well as helping students to identify which topics they have to focus more on. We do this by setting weekly homework assignments that are graded and shared with parents so they are also informed about their child's progress.

Online tutoring allows for a more flexible schedule for both students and tutors as well as providing resources that allow to track all the work done in the class automatically and in such a way that it can be accessed at all times. That way, students can focus fully on the content being explained rather than rushing to write things down and not really understanding what they are writing down.

Understanding the concepts being learnt is a far more rewarding experience than simply memorizing them. Thus, we always reinforce the idea of understanding the process by analyzing the topics from the earliest foundations and making a mental map connecting different ideas. By also using exam mark schemes students begin to identify key concepts and observe the whole process of answering a question and retaining knowledge.

Special Requirements

At The Tutoring Collective, we believe that every child is unique, and their learning experience should be just as individualized. For students with ADHD, anxiety, and dyslexia and other learning differences, we offer specialized tutoring programs tailored to meet their specific needs and help them thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.
